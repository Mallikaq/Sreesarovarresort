Amenities & Activities

Stay, celebrate, and explore Chikkaballapur.

Use Sree Sarovar Resort for peaceful 24-hour stays, family visits, open function hall bookings, event lawn gatherings, and quick access to nearby attractions.

Family Stay

Open Function Hall

Open hall for small events at ₹30,000 per event, suitable for around 150-200 seated guests.

Event Lawn

Seating, decoration, and food can be arranged by guests or coordinated from our side with extra charges.
